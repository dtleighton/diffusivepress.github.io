The main points of the lecture were
  • Concentration Polarization
  • Taylor Dispersion for Simple Shear Flow with Polarization
  • Asymmetric Flow Field Flow Fractionation (AF4)

Goals:

After this class you should be able to:
  • Calculate the thickness of a concentration polarization layer.
  • Understand how Taylor Dispersion results from polarization and shear.
  • Calculate the design criteria for an AF4 separation device.
