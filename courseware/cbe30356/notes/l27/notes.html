Class notes

Read through pages 384-397 of the notes and view the online narration below. Don't forget to complete the quiz in Canvas!

The main points of the lecture were
  • Electroosmosis
  • Capillary Electrophoresis
  • Streaming Potential

Goals:

After this class you should be able to:
  • Calculate the electroosmotic velocity resulting from a charged surface and a tangential electric field.
  • Understand how electrophoresis can be used to separate species based on their electrophoretic mobility.
  • Calculate the current resulting from an imposed shear flow tangential to a charged surface.
