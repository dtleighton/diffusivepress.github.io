Problem of the Day:

    In this problem we examine the scaling of transport equations: how to eliminate terms which are identically zero and scale out terms which are just "small" in the region of interest. As an example we shall look at a simple problem from fluid mechanics: the squishing of a volume of viscous fluid between two plates under constant force, predicting how the radius grows with time.
