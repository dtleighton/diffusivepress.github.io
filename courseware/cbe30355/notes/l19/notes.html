Demonstration:

    How blowing on a plate can actually lead to suction. While the example in class is rather whimsical, the Bernoulli effect illustrated by it is of importance in many applications. As an example, Bernoulli suction forms the basis of contactless grippers which can be used to pick up fragile objects.
