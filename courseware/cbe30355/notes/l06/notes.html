    Demonstration:

      Hero's Fountain:
      In class today we demonstrated "Hero's Fountain", an example of hydrostatics which goes back to Hero of Alexandria over two millenia ago - fluids research has been going on a long time! To learn more about this early fluid mechanic read the translation of his work from Greek given here. The Editor's and Translator's Prefaces are also interesting. The version of Hero's Fountain demonstrated in class is given in Section 23, where the outlet of the "wine" is used as the inlet to the "water" container. Note that the relative height of the two vessels doesn't matter, which is what produces the interesting effect!
